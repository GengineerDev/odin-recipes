Tom's Scrambled Egg

Description

It is an amazing recipe made by Tom's dad.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, milk, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Heat a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Cook eggs in hot skillet without stirring until set on the bottom, about 1 1/2 minutes. Flip eggs and cook without stirring until set on the other side, about 1 minute more. Remove from heat.
  3. Spread 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise on each piece of toast. Spread mustard over mayonnaise onto each piece of toast. Place eggs on one piece of toast and top with remaining toast.
